Here's a sample application for sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Manager's Name]
[Company Name]
[Company Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Subject: Application for Sick Leave

Dear [Manager's Name],

I am writing to request a day's sick leave on [Date of Leave] as I am currently unwell and unable to attend work. I have been experiencing [briefly mention the symptoms or illness].

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on my work as soon as I am feeling better. I will provide a doctor's certificate if required.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

Note: You can modify the application as per your needs and company's policies. It's always a good idea to keep a doctor's certificate handy, just in case.
